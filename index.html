Our Approach

We help individuals gain in-demand skills and help institutions scale training through our AI-enabled system, content, and support.

System – tenx, our scalable platform, delivers personalized learning at scale with real-time insights on learner progress and outcomes.

Content – Hands-on, industry-aligned training in AI, data, and core career skills mapped directly to job market needs across Africa.

Support – End-to-end guidance from onboarding to job search, including tutor support, community engagement, and AI-powered job matching.

AI4E – AI for Employment

Creating an AI-ready youth workforce using an AI-enabled platform

AI4E prepares 1M+ African learners for AI-ready careers at scale through the tenx platform by identifying talent gaps, delivering personalized, market-aligned training, and providing contextualized learning for university students, early career professionals, and disadvantaged youth including those in rural areas and outside formal education to build AI literacy and career readiness for meaningful employment.

  • Economic Impact: $360M in projected lifetime earnings across 1M learners
  • Unit Cost: <$30 per learner for AI-driven training and placement support
  • Platform Capacity: Scalable to 100K+ users in parallel

No cost to join. Institutions can participate by signing a non-binding expression of interest.
